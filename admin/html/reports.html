REPORTS

  • • Company Snapshot - overall view of the company and performance


  • • A/R Detail - breakdown of customer accounts by current, 30, 60, and 90 days


  • • Revenue Report - summary of revenue by rep, invoice date, including cost and profit margin


  • • Carrier Volume - total number of shipments, cost and profit margin by carrier


  • • Quotes vs Shipments - comparison of quotes that have not shipped within specified time period


  • • Shipment Services - summary of shipment services by pickup date


  • • Customer List - list of customers by rep and/or profitability
